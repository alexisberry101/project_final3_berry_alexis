About

History

Nancy's Bakery & Cafe was founded in 2018 by Nancy. After receiving a lot of praise over her challah bread, Nancy decided to start making the bread for her freinds and family. The amount of people who enjoy her bread continued to grow. After retiring from her job as a nurse, Nancy decided to open a bakery where she could sell her bread along with other yummy goodies. Nancy's is a place where people can come together for a morning coffee or an afternoon lunch. We even offer hot items that are great for dinner! We look forward to welcoming you to Nancy's Bakery & Cafe during your next visit.

Location

Nancy's is located in the heart of Miami at 1111 Brickell Bay Drive Miami, Florida 33131. For, a more detailed map visit the contact page. We are open every day of the week from 7am-3pm.
